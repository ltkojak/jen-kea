The Kea DHCP Management Console

A full-featured web management interface for ISC Kea DHCP Server, built as a practical alternative to ISC Stork for homelab and small infrastructure deployments.

Jen provides lease management, static reservations, subnet editing, Telegram alerts, audit logging, and more — all from a browser-based UI.
